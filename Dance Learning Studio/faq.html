FAQ

How do I get started?
Visit our website and sign up for a class or membership that suits your level and goals.
Why should I learn to dance?
Dancing improves physical fitness, mental well-being, coordination, and is a great way to socialize.
What is the fastest way to become a better dancer?
Consistent practice, feedback from instructors, and attending regular classes are key.
Is there an age limit or range?
Absolutely not! We welcome all ages, from young children to seniors.
Do I need a partner?
No, we rotate partners in class and also accommodate solo dancers.
What should I wear for class?
Comfortable clothes that allow movement. Athletic wear or dance attire works great.
Do I need dance shoes?
Dance shoes are recommended but not required for beginners.
How long does it take to learn to dance?
It depends on your goals and commitment, but you can see progress in just a few weeks.
Group classes vs private lessons?
Group classes are great for learning with others; private lessons offer personalized feedback and faster growth.
What styles of dance do you offer?
We offer ballroom, Latin, hip-hop, jazz, contemporary, and more.
Can I achieve fitness goals through dance?
Absolutely! Dance improves cardiovascular health, muscle tone, and endurance.
Do you offer any specialized training?
Yes, we provide competitive dance training, performance coaching, and workshops.
Do you accept people with disabilities?
Yes, our classes are inclusive and we accommodate all dancers to feel welcome and supported.
What is your cancellation policy?
You may cancel 24 hours in advance for a full refund or class credit.
